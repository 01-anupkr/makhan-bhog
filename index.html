About Makhan Bhog

Shah's Brothers – A Legacy of Trust

Makhan Bhog by Shah's Brothers is a premium flour mill providing top quality Atta (flour), Rice, and general grocery items. We cater to both wholesale and retail customers with equal dedication.

Our state-of-the-art flour mill processes the finest wheat grains to produce soft, nutritious, and aromatic flour that makes every roti, paratha and puri taste heavenly. We also stock a wide range of rice varieties and everyday grocery essentials.
