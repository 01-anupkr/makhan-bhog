About Makhan Bhog

Shah's Brothers – A Legacy of Trust

Makhan Bhog by Shah's Brothers is a premium flour mill & wholesale general store providing top quality Atta (flour), Rice, Dals, Spices, Oil, Ghee and all everyday grocery items. We cater to both wholesale and retail customers with equal dedication — order everything from atta to masala in one go!

Our state-of-the-art flour mill processes the finest wheat grains to produce soft, nutritious, and aromatic flour that makes every roti, paratha and puri taste heavenly. Our general store stocks a complete range of grocery essentials so you can place combo orders in a single delivery.
